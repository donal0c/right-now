# SPEC_EXERCISES.md

## Exercise Library — Micro-Interventions (v1)

---

## 1. Physiological Sigh
ID: phys_sigh

Purpose: Rapid calming

Protocol:
- Inhale nose
- Short second inhale
- Long slow exhale mouth
- Repeat

Default: 60s

---

## 2. Paced Breathing (6 bpm)
ID: paced_breath

Protocol:
- Inhale 4s
- Exhale 6s

Default: 2m

---

## 3. 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
ID: ground_54321

Protocol:
- 5 see
- 4 feel
- 3 hear
- 2 smell
- 1 taste/breath

Default: 2m

---

## 4. Cognitive Defusion
ID: defusion_thought

Protocol:
- Identify thought
- Say: “I’m having the thought that…”
- Repeat

Default: 90s

---

## 5. Panoramic Gaze
ID: panoramic_gaze

Protocol:
- Relax eyes
- Widen field of view
- No focus

Default: 60s

---

## 6. Progressive Muscle Release
ID: pmr_quick

Protocol:
- Shoulders tense/release
- Hands tense/release
- Jaw tense/release

Default: 2m

---

## State Mapping
- Anxious (panicky) → phys_sigh
- Anxious (ruminating) → ground_54321
- Overwhelmed → paced_breath
- Angry (hot) → paced_breath
- Angry (stewing) → defusion_thought
- Foggy (can’t start) → defusion_thought
- Foggy (can’t focus) → panoramic_gaze
- Overstimulated → panoramic_gaze
- Tense → pmr_quick
